Tampa Bay Lightning

by Taso Halverson

So, what's been going on with the lightning?

The Tampa Bay Lightning is a prestigous ice hockey team that suprisingly has alot of young players as well as old. The bolts were founded in 1992 which is considered a young team because that is fairly recent compared to the other teams in the National Hockey League (NHL).

The Lightning are from Tampa, Florida. Their home games take place at the notorius Amalie Arena. The general manager of the team is Julien BriseBois while the owner is Jeffrey Vinik. October 7th, 1992 was their first season game and many were suprised to find out this new team of youngsters beat the well-established and powerful blackhawks for their first season game. Fans were slow to support the Lightning because in 1992 they did not play at the Amalie arena and their home field only sat 11,000 people.

This season the lightning had the best record in the league for the eastern conference. However, fans were disappointed after the bolts lost in the first round of stanley cup to a team who had one of the worst records of the season, The Blue Jackets.
