Overview

Real Madrid is a professional club soccer team in Madrid, Spain. Their manager, Santiago Solari, is a retired professional soccer player that played for Atletico Madrid, Real madrid, and Inter. He was made head coach in November, last year and has led the team to 14 wins, 3 draws, and only 6 losses.

Some of Real Madrids players include the famous Christiano Ronoldo, Luca Modric, Sergio Ramos, Gareth Bale, and the forward of the team Benzema. This group of players together have won four Champions League trophies, two LaLiga titles, two Copa del Rey trophies, three Club World Cups, three UEFA Super Cups and two Supercopa de Espana trophies.

The Santiago Bernabéu Stadium is located in Madrid, Spain and is the Real Madrid home stadium. It was built in 1947 with a current seating capacity of 81,044.

Real Madrid supporters are called Madridistas, Los Merenges, Los Blancos or even the White Army. Soccer in their city spans back 100's of years and is taken as some can argue, religiously.
